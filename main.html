Varanasi (Banaras), Uttar Pradesh


Varanasi, also known as Banaras or Kashi, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. Located on the banks of the holy River Ganga, it is the spiritual capital of India and a major center for Hinduism, Buddhism, culture, music, and learning. The city is famous for its ghats, temples, evening Ganga aarti, and vibrant street life.

Top 5 attractions

  1. Kashi Vishwanath Temple
  2. Dashashwamedh Ghat
  3. Manikarnika Ghat
  4. Sarnath
  5. Banaras Hindu University (BHU)

Must-try foods

  • Kachori Sabzi
  • Banarasi Paan
  • Malaiyo
  • Tamatar Chaat
  • Lassi (especially at Blue Lassi Shop)
